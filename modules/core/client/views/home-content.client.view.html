We let you animate your QR codes.

ACME (Animated Codes Made Easy LLC) enables you to create high quality animations of your QR codes. You can create animated QR codes with ACME’s online QR code generator, or you can work directly with ACME to create animated codes tailored to your exact specifications. ACME is a catalyst for reshaping the unattractive static QR code into an enticing alternative, embedded with your company’s branding, logo, or campaign.

Animated QR codes fully utilize your electronic signage capital investment with eye catching ‘call to action’ scan targets for people.

Create a cool animated QR code in under 5 minutes!

Traditional QR codes are a portal from any real place to any digital place. The digital place can provide:
- Practical information to the participant (helpful URLS to locations, ticketing, surveys)
- Usage statistics (such as times and places scanned) to the provider
- Valuable information to the participant (digital coupons to encourage commerce)
- Actual monetary value (Bitcoin, Ethereum)


Animated QR codes can significantly enhance the value of a QR code by:
- Catching the attention of the scanning participant
- Clearly communicating the value within the code to the scanning participant
- Communicating the authenticity of the code to the scanning participant

ACME provides two products in the 'Software as a Service' (SaaS) model:
- Clients can make codes interactively using ACME's online QR code generator, or
- Clients requiring large volume and/or automated code creation can use our documented public Web API portal.
These products support creation of animated codes from anywhere in the world 24/7/365.
